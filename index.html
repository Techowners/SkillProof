How SkillProof Works

A simple three-step process to mint, manage, and showcase your verifiable credentials.

1

Connect Your Wallet

Link your Ethereum wallet (like MetaMask) to create your decentralized identity.

2

Mint Your Skills

Upload proof of your skills through assessments or code repositories to mint credentials.

3

Share & Verify

Share your profile with employers who can instantly verify your credentials on-chain.

Why Choose SkillProof?

The future of verifiable credentials is decentralized, transparent, and user-owned.

Own Your Data

Traditional platforms own and control your credential data. With SkillProof, you maintain complete ownership through your crypto wallet.

Interoperable

Your credentials can be used across multiple platforms, job boards, and metaverse environments without vendor lock-in.

Employer Trust

Companies can instantly verify the authenticity of your credentials on-chain, reducing fraud and increasing trust.

Future-Proof

As Web3 adoption grows, your blockchain-based credentials will become increasingly valuable in the decentralized economy.
